Questions, answers

What makes InsightAIQ different?

We focus on harnessing AI, knowledge graphs, and blockchain to simplify complex data challenges, improve decision-making, and identify investment opportunities.

What problem does InsightPII solve for businesses?

InsightPII resolves issues arising from multiple fragmented data sources capturing the same entity differently. It intelligently merges these varying records into a single, reliable master record. InsightPII uses propreitary embedding combined with state of the art algorithms to accurately match and unify data, ensuring businesses have authoritative, consistent records across all systems.

What's unique about Whitespots?

Whitespots constructs a comprehensive knowledge graph from multiple news and research streams to predict the ripple effects of technological and market developments, enabling early identification of investment opportunities. Here is an example: if a new element is discovered for battery production, our knowledge graph assesses how this discovery impacts industries like electric vehicles and cobalt supply chains, highlighting investment opportunities before the market fully reacts.
