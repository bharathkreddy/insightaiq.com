Whitespots

A powerful predictive intelligence platform that transforms diverse data streams into actionable investment insights. By mapping and analyzing complex relationships and trends across industries, Whitespots identifies early signals and hidden opportunities, enabling smarter and earlier investment decisions.

Dynamic Knowledge Graphs

Whitespots leverages advanced AI to construct dynamic knowledge graphs from your organization's chosen data sources, complemented by industry-specific recommendations from us. This creates a comprehensive, interconnected view of entities, markets, and technologies, providing unparalleled clarity on potential investment impacts.

Proactive Investment Recommendations

Our platform continuously analyzes your curated data streams to surface actionable investment opportunities well before they become market consensus. Each recommendation is clearly explained, offering detailed insights into the underlying factors and predicted market movements.

Personalized Watchlists

Whitespots lets you define personalized watchlists of entities or sectors crucial to your investment strategy. It proactively monitors and reports on these entities, assessing and highlighting how emerging trends and events across other domains may influence their future.
